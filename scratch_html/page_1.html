Key Benefits of Cordyceps Capsules
1. Enhanced Energy and Athletic Performance
Cordyceps is well-known for its ability to reduce fatigue and boost stamina. 
ATP Production: It is thought to increase the body's production of adenosine triphosphate (ATP), which is essential for delivering energy to the muscles. 
Oxygen Utilization: Some studies suggest it can improve VO2 max (the maximum amount of oxygen your body can use during exercise), making it popular among endurance athletes. 
2. Immune System Support
It acts as an immunomodulator, meaning it can help balance the immune system.
It may stimulate specific immune cells, such as Natural Killer (NK) cells and macrophages, which help the body fight off infections and abnormal cells. 
3. Anti-Aging and Longevity
The high antioxidant content in Cordyceps helps neutralize free radicals, which contribute to cellular aging.

Kidney Function: In TCM, Cordyceps is a primary herb for supporting kidney health. Some clinical studies have shown improved renal function in patients with chronic kidney disease. 
Heart Health: It may help regulate cholesterol levels and support cardiovascular health by improving blood flow and reducing inflammation.

Concentration: Extraction (usually using hot water or alcohol) breaks down the tough chitin walls of the fungus, making the active compounds like beta-glucans and cordycepin more bioavailable.

Taste: Cordyceps has a very earthy, slightly bitter flavor that many find unpleasant in tea or smoothies; capsules bypass this entirely.

Source: Look for supplements that specify the species (typically Cordyceps sinensis or Cordyceps militaris) and mention "fruiting body" or "dual-extract" for the highest quality.

Key Potential Benefits

Frequent urination (especially at night). 
Difficulty starting or maintaining a steady urine flow. 
The sensation of an incomplete bladder emptying.

Common Side Effects Mild digestive issues (nausea, diarrhea), dizziness, or headaches. Taking it with food can help.
