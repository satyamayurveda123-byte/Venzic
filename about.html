Company Profile

Venzic Lifesciences Pvt. Ltd.

Established as a Limited Company in the year 2025 at Bhiwani (Haryana, India), Venzic Lifesciences Pvt. Ltd. has emerged as a leading manufacturer and contract partner for high-potency Ayurvedic and pharmaceutical formulations.

We specialize in third-party contract manufacturing of a wide range of products including **Pharmaceutical Capsules, Cream, Oil, Chyawanprash, Himalayan Shilajit, Shilajit Honey Jar Sticks, and Swarnaprash Extra Power Powder**. We supply premium formulations designed to meet international purity standards for brands worldwide.

Under the leadership of our mentor, **Mr. Sanjay**, who has immense experience and business insight, Venzic Lifesciences is dedicated to producing high-quality health items. Due to his carefully devised quality policies, we have built a huge list of clients across different parts of the world.
